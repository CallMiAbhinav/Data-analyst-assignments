Step 1:Login to "app.powerbi.com" and login with organization mail id.
Step 2:In the left pane click on "Workspaces" then click "New Workspace".
Step 3:In a create workspace form fill in details that includes name of workspace, description, 
  upload image if you want, then click on Advanced then in the license mode select the kind of
  license that the organization using.
Step 4:In the Default Storage Format select the kind of dataset storage format either big or small
Step 5:Click on Apply. New workspace created.
Step 6:Upload the content into the workspace 
Step 7:Click on the "Manage Access" then click on "Add people or groups" add people according to the 
need with their roles and access they required as per the project.
